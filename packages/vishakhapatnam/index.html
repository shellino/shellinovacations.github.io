Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Vishakhapatnam

Welcome to Vishakhaptnam. Today we will have a 'Introductory' session with our guests. In the evening we will visit Ramkrishna Beach and Submarine Museum.

Day 2: Vishakhapatnam - Araku Valley

Today we will move towards Araku Valley, a Prominent hill station with Tribal majority. Enroute we will visit Borra Caves.In the evening we will visit the Coffee Factory. Today we will enjoy the local tribal Dance.

Day 3: Araku Valley

Today we will visit the Araku Tribal Museum and Padmapuram Gardens. We will proceed towards Vishakhapatnam post lunch. Enroute we will see the Anathgiri Coffee Plantations. Overnight in Vizag.

Day 4: Vishakhapatnam

Today we will visit Lawsons Bay, Kailash Giri, Rushikonda Beach, VUDA Park. Eveniong spent at your lesuire.

Day 5: Vizag - Departure to hometown

After breakfast we will move for return journey.Tour concludes
