Valley of Flowers

7 days / 6 nights
Uttarakhand

Night stays

Haridwar - 1 nights
Govindghat - 1 nights
Ghangaria/Auli - 4 nights

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Delhi - Haridwar

Reach Delhi and thereafter proceed towards Haridwar. Spend the evening at leisure. Over dinner, we would be having an introduction and discussing the course of action over the next week.

Day 2: Haridwar - Auli

The great journey begins. Travel the glorious route from Haridwar to Joshimath. Overnight at the picturesque Auli / Govindghat.

Day 3: Ghangaria

Today, we climb till Ghangaria, which is the base for our future forays. This trek is an excellent photographic opportunity, with some breathtaking scenery to offer! Overnight in Ghangaria.

Day 4: Valley of Flowers

Today is the D-day! Spend an entire day in the fabled Valley of Flowers. Flowers, glaciers, mountains, meadows and your camera!

Day 5: Hemkund Sahib

Trek the enchanting route to Hemkund Sahib. This is a superb place for making some glorious images of the highmountains, devout Sikhs and the elusive Brahmakamal.

Day 6: Govind Ghat

We visit the valley once more! ( There is an option of descending the mountain and visiting Badrinath instead ). Overnight in Govindghat / Auli.

Day 7: Haridwar - Delhi

Today, we retrace our steps back to Haridwar and later board the overnight train to Delhi. Tour Concludes.
