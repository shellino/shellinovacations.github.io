Kashmir Amarnath Vaishnodevi
Walk with the nature.

Kashmir Amarnath Vaishnodevi

9 days / 8 nights
Jammu & Kashmir

Night stays

Srinagar (House Boat) - 2 nights
Srinagar (Hotel) - 2 nights
Sonmarg - nights
Baltal - 2 nights
Amarnath | Gulmarg | Katra - 2 nights

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Srinagar

Arrive at Srinagar, report to coach and proceed to the house boat.

Day 2: Srinagar - Gulmarg - Srinagar

Visit Gulmarg - the "Ski slopes of Kashmir", also known as "Meadow of Flowers". Enjoy longest cable car ride from Gulmarg to Affarwat (Khilanmarg) snow point.

Day 3: Srinagar - Sonamarg - Baltal

Proceed to Baltal. En-route visit Sonamarg known as 'Meadow of Gold' on the valley of River Jhelum . View magnificent picturesque mountain ranges. Arrive at Baltal in the evening.

Day 4: Baltal - Amarnath - Baltal

Visit Amarnath - a Holy Abode of Lord Shiva. Each year this yatra attracts thousands of pilgrims.

Day 5: Baltal - Srinagar

Proceed to Srinagar. On arrival free time for relaxation and shopping.

Day 6: Srinagar

Visit Shankaracharya hills and holy temple of Lord Shiva located on top of a hill. Enjoy Dal lake Shikara ride. Visit Charchinar Island, Shalimar Bagh -'Abode of Love' and Nishad Bagh - 'The garden of pleasure'.

Day 7: Srinagar - Katra

Proceed to Katra. In the evening arrive at Katra - the base point for Vaishnodevi.

Day 8: Katra - Vaishnodevi

Early morning proceed for Vaishno Devi mandir one of the holiest hindu temples dedicated to shakti.

Day 9: Katra - Jammu

Proceed to Jammu. Board the train/flight for your hometown. Tour Concludes.
