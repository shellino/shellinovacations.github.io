Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Leh

Fly into Leh. Rest and acclimatize. Excited, nervous and ecstatic, you will fly over the magnificient Himalayan mountains and reach Leh. Today, we will take it easy and rest at the hotel. In the evening, we will go for a short stroll in the Leh market, do some shopping and just take in the amazing vibe of this beautiful mountain town. Overnight in Leh.

Day 2: Leh

Exploring Leh. Getting ready for the ultimate adventure. Riding distance: 40-50 kms. Being properly acclimatized is of paramount importance in the high mountains. Keeping this in mind, we will not go on a long ride today but instead check out the local attractions in Leh. The mysterious Leh palace, a few ancient monasteries and the ethereal Shanti Stupa are all monuments of spectacular brilliance. We will ride to all these Buddhist masterpieces, eat some delectable Ladakhi food and just have a gala time in Leh town. Over dinner, we will discuss the strategy and plan of action for the upcoming adventure. Overnight in Leh.

Day 3: Leh

Exploring the monastic trail and the moonland. Riding distance: 250 kms. Our first major ride of the expedition! We rev our way towards the breathtaking Lamayuru monastery, one of Ladakh's finest! On our way to Lamayuru we drive past ancient Buddhist relics, gargantuan mountains and deep valleys and along the fabled waters of the Indus. With muchdeserved stops at Alchi, Likir and the Sangam (confluence of the Zanskar and Indus), we ride to our heart's fill! Just before the Lamayuru monastery is a place called the 'moonland', and, if you hear closely, you will find such utter peace and tranquil that you have never experienced before! (The moonland is so named because of it striking resemblance to the moon's surface). Overnight in Leh.

Day 4: Leh - Hundar

Up the Khardungla and into the pretty Nubra. Riding distance: 110 kms. It is time to ride up the world's highest motorable road (for civilian traffic)! This is every rider's ultimate dream! Standing tall and proud atop the pass and drinking a contented cup of tea is one of the greatest pleasures in life! Beyond the imposing Khardungla pass and towards the north is this region of astounding beauty called the Nubra valley. Embraced by the Nubra and Shyok rivers and home to the high-altitude desert, this is a place which leaves many a traveler in fits of joy! In the evening, as the orange sun sets against the stark Ladakhi mountains, we will go on a camel ride on 79 the double-humped Bactrian camels at the Hundar desert! At night, the cold and crisp wind blowing over from the desolate north would make the campfire even more enjoyable! Overnight in Hundar.

Day 5: Hundar - Leh

Tales of Maitreya and the magic of the Nubra. Riding distance: 110 kms. After a thrilling and adventurous day 4, we rest our tired bodies a bit more. After a leisurely breakfast in the lap of the mighty mountains of Diskit, we move towards the grand monastery there. The tall and imposing statue of the Maitreya Buddha at the monastery, overlooking the entire valley, is a sight to behold! After exploring the magnificent monastery, we say goodbye to the Nubra valley and prepare to climb the Khardungla once more! In the evening, as Leh gets ready to plunge into another pleasant night, we stroll through the market and take in its happygo- lucky vibe! The interested ones can ride up to the Shanti Stupa and wonder at the number of stars that can be seen from earth (we might even see a band of the MilkyWay galaxy)! Overnight in Leh.

Day 6: Leh - Pangong

The glory of the Pangong. Riding distance: 160 kms. Immortalized by Bollywood, adorned by Nature, the Pangong is Ladakh's gift to us! On our agenda today is riding up the breathtakingly beautiful Chang la pass, which is a true test of good riding, unbridled passion and ultimate love for the outdoors! The occasional snow and gravel on top of the Chang la might make it an even more adventurous ride! On the other side of the Changla are the beautiful Changthang meadows beyond which, like a glistening jewel, is the Pangong! We will have a sumptuous hot lunch at our campsite located on the lake's banks, take in the beauty of the Pangong and go for a leisurely ride along the water! The sight of the evening sun setting against those differently hued mountains would bowl you over! Sometimes at night, the water shines in the starlight as if it is molten silver! Overnight in Pangong

Day 7: Pahalgam - Katra - Vaishnodevi

Welcome to Katra, the base point of Vaishnodevi. Today we start the trek of Vaishnodevi Temple (a total trek of 28 kms).

Day 8: Vaishnodevi - Katra

Today we arrive from Vaishnodevi. We have free Day for relaxation or leisure activities. Enjoy get together with your travel companions in the evening.

Day 9: Katra - Departure from Jammu

It is time now to say goodbye to our tour companions. Let us be in contact with each other through Social Media, calls…. And meet again on one more memorable tour. See you all..!! Tour concludes.
