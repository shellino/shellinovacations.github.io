Jagannath Puri Kolkata Gangasagar
City of the Ratha-Yatra

Jagannath Puri Kolkata Gangasagar

8 days / 7 nights
West Bengal, Orissa.

Night stays

Kolkata( Gangasagar | Shantiniketan ) - 4 nights
Bhubaneshwar - 2 nights
Puri - 1 nights

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Kolkata

Arrive in Kolkata, report to the coach and proceed to hotel.

Day 2: Kolkata - Gangasagar - Kolkata

Visit Gangasagar where the Ganaga flows into the Bay of Bengal and largest number of devotees gather in the Ganga Sagar mela & visit Kapil Muni temple.

Day 3: Kolkata

Visit jain temple, Belurmath, Dakshineshwar Kali Temple, Victoria Memorial and pass by Howrah Bridge - The bridge is a famous symbol of Kolkata. City orientation includes Dalhousie Square, Fort Williams, Secretariat Building and Old Court House Street.

Day 4: Kolkata - Shantiniketan - Kolkata

Visit Shantiniketan - Dehali, Prayer hall, Shantiniketan griha & Bichitra.

Day 5: Kolkata - Bhubaneshwar

Departure to Bhubaneshwar by train ( Guest have to book Kolkata to Bhubaneshwar train on their own). Arrive at Bhubaneshwar. Enjoy shopping in the evening.

Day 6: Bhubaneshwar - Puri

Proceed to Puri. Visit Lord Jagannath Temple dedicated to Lord Vishnu and Gundecha Temple - Aunt of Lord Jagannath. Evening free to stroll around Puri beach and shopping.

Day 7: Puri - Konark - Bhubaneshwar

Proceed to Bhubaneshwar. En-route enjoy shopping at Pipli and visit Dhauli - it is presumed to be the area where Kalinga War was fought. See the famous Shanti Stupa and inscription carved by King Ashoka. En-route visit Konark Sun Temple - a World Heritage Site.

Day 8: Bhubaneshwar - Mumbai

Visit ancient temples like Raja Rani Temple, Lingaraj Temple - 54mts high temple dedicated to Lord Tribhuvaneshwara, Mukteshwar temple often referred as the "Gem of Orissa Architecture". Board the train to your hometown. Tour Concludes.
