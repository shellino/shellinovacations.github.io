Nagzira - 3 nights

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Nagpur - Nagzira

On our arrival in Nagpur, we have a sumptuous breakfast at a south Indian restaurant before heading towards Nagzira. We check-in to the resort and following a nice lunch, start with our introductions and orientation. In the evening, as the cold sets in, we explore the surrounding jungle for birds, boars and deer! Our last group saw a sloth bear on this very evening trail!

Day 2: Nagzira

Today morning, we set out for our first jungle safari. The magnificent jungle would soon unfold its many stories in frontof you! Be ready, be inspired! After the safari we stay in the forest and check-in to the fabled Nagzira Lake Rest house. Now, you are in the most pristine wild in the midst of absolute beauty! After lunch, we go for an evening safari and try our luck in searching for all the species that evaded us on the morning safari. At night, as alarm calls and jungle sounds start getting more prominent, we go for a short night walk. This is thrilling, believe us! Also, the night sky in Nagzira is gorgeous, we will spend some time shooting star trails and the Milky Way!

Day 3: Nagzira

Today we go on two more safaris. If we are lucky, we would have already spotted the tiger by now, and hope to spot other elusive animals.

Day 4: Nagzira - Nagpur

In the form of the morning safari today, we go on our last safari on the camp. This would be our final chance to live the Nagzira life, make stunning images of the morning rays permeating through the verdant greens and try and get a glimpse of those creatures dearest to us! And like all good things, we will bid goodbye to Nagzira post lunch and make our way towards Nagpur for return journey. Tour Concludes.
