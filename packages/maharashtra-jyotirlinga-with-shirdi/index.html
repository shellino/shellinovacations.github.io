Maharashtra Jyotirlinga with Shirdi

6 days / 5 nights
Maharashtra

Night stays

Bhimashankar (Ahmednagar) - 1 nights
Ambajogai - 1 nights
Aurangabad (Parali Vajinath | Aundha Nagnath) - 2 nights
Shirdi (Trimbakeshwar) - 1 nights

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Pune - Bhimashankar - Ahmednagar

Early morning proceed from Pune to Bhimashankar. Visit Bhimashankar an ancient shrine situated in the Sahyadri hills. It is one of the well known jyotirlinga amongst the 12 jyotirlingas. After Darshan proceed to Ahmednagar. Arrive at Ahmednagar in the evening.

Day 2: Ahmednagar - Parali Vajinath - Ambajogai

Early morning proceed to Ambajogai - a heritage temple dedicated to Goddess Yogeshwari. Later visit Parali Vajinath Jyotirling temple. Arrive at Ambajogai in the evening.

Day 3: Ambajogai - Aundha Nagnath - Aurangabad

Proceed to Aurangabad. Enroute visit Aundha Naganath Jyotirling Temple. Arrive at Aurangabad in the evening.

Day 4: Aurangabad

Aurangabad city tour includes Grishneshwar Temple- one of the jyotirlingas, Later visit Ellora caves, Bhadra Maruti temple - the only sleeping Maruti idol in India, Pawanchakki and Bibi ka Maqbara.

Day 5: Aurangabad - Shirdi

Proceed to Shirdi. Evening visit Sai Baba Temple.

Day 6: Departure from Shirdi

Proceed to pune. Enroute visit trimbakeshwar - one of the 12 jyotirlingas. Tour concludes.
