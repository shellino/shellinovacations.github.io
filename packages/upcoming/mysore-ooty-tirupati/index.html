Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Mysore

Welcome to Mysore. We will have short 'Introductory' session after breakfast. Today we will visit the places like Mysore Palace, Botanical Garden and St. Philomena’s Church, evening spent at your leisure.

Day 2: Mysore

Today we will visit the famous Chamundi Mata Temple at Chamundi Hills. Post lunch we will move towards Shrirangpatnam village and will visit the famous Brindavan Garden in the evening and will watch the laser show.

Day 3: Mysore - Ooty

Today in the morning we will proceed towards ooty. Enjoy the breathtaking sceneries of Bandipur and Mudumalai National Park in the Nilgiri Hills. In the evening we will visit the Ooty Lake, Botanical Garden, Shooting Point, Pykara Lake and Local Market.

Day 4: Ooty - Coonoor - Ooty

We will go to Coonoor famous for its natural beauty. We will visit Dodabetta Peak, Tea Factory, Dolphin Nose, and Lamb’s Rock. Evening spent at your leisure and we will enjoy Toy train journey.

Day 5: Ooty - Coimbatore - Tirupati

After Breakfast we will proceed towards Coimbatore and board the train for Tirupati, whole day journey towards Tirupati.

Day 6: Tirupati

We will visit the world famous Tirupati Balaji Temple and visit places like Zoological Park, Ttd Gardens.

Day 7: Tirupati - Gudur

We will proceed for return journey towards our hometown from Gudur Junction. Tour ends with happy memories.
