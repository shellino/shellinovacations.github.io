Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Goa

Welcome to Goa the coolest place in India. Evening free for relaxation. Today we have a short 'Introductory' session before dinner.

Day 2: North Goa

Welcome to North Goa which include Panjim, the capital of Goa. Today we explore Famous churches of old Goa which reflect the richness of Greco- Roman architectural style. We Visit Dona Paula, Baga Beach, Calangute Beach, and Fort Aguada overlooking the beach. Later We enjoy a boat cruise and admire the sunset views.

Day 3: South Goa

Welcome to South Goa. Today we visit Shri Mangesh Temple in Madgaon, Shri Shanta Durga Temple dedicated to Goddess of peace, Shanteri. In the evening enjoy get together with your travel companions.

Day 4: Departure from Goa

It is time now to say goodbye to our tour companions. Let us be in contact with each other through Social Media and meet again on one more memorable tour. See you all.!! Tour concludes.
