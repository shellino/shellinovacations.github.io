Madhya Pradesh Memories

Madhya Pradesh Memories

4 days / 3 nights
Madhya Pradesh

Night stays

Kanha - 1 nights
Panchmarhi - 1 nights

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Jabalpur - Kanha

Guest will meet at Jabalpur & will proceed towards Beda ghat. Today we visit the famous marble Rocks & Water fall at Bedaghat. Later we will depart towards Kanha National park after Lunch.Overnight stay will be at Kanha.

Day 2: Kanha - Panchmarhi

In the morning, we will visit the world famous Kanha National park. Afternoon spend leisure time at hotel on your own. After dinner departure towards Panchmarhi, whole night journey.

Day 3: Panchmarhi

Today we explore panchmarhi a famous hill station in ranges of satpuda & will visit famous Dhupgarh, chauragarh, Panarpani & Pandav caves.

Day 4: Panchmarhi - Departure to hometown

Today we explore Panchmarhi with silver fall & water fall. After Lunch we will proceed for return journey.
