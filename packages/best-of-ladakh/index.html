Life is a Journey. Complete It.

Best of Ladakh

8 days / 7 nights
Jammu & Kashmir

Night stays

Leh - 5 nights
Turtuk - 1 nights
Pangong Lake - 1 nights

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Leh

Arrive Leh. Transfer to the hotel for some rest followed by an evening visit to the Shanti Stupa, Leh palace and the Leh bazaar. Overnight in Leh.

Day 2: Leh - Lamayuru

Travel the glorious route from Leh to Lamayuru and back. Visit the Lamayuru, Alchi, Likir and Ridzong monasteries and spend time photographing the unique land formations here. This part of Ladakh is known as Moonland. Visit the confluence of the Indus and Zanskar rivers on the way back. Overnight in Leh.

Day 3: Leh - Nubra Valley - Leh

Travel to the picturesque Nubra valley. Made famous as a high-altitude desert, Nubra valley houses some of Ladakh`s best treasures. We go via the Khardung La pass on the way, the world`s highest motorable road. Explore the Diskit monastery and spend time on a camel safari at Hundar ( a desert at 10000 ft above sea level ). By evening, we will reach Turtuk, the last village in Ladakh ( and India ) and one of the 3 Balti villages left in India. This is an extraordinary cultural experience which coupled with the enchanting mountain views would leave you spellbound. Overnight in Turtuk.

Day 4: Leh - Turtuk - Leh

Spend the morning exploring Turtuk`s apricot fields, old Balti houses and interacting with the locals. Still pretty much untouched by tourism, this is one of the most authentic Ladakhi experiences. We trace our way back to Leh to reach in time for a sumptuous hot dinner.

Day 5: Leh - Pangong

It is Pangong day at last! A stunningly beautiful high-altitude lake nestled amidst the Ladakhi mountains, Pangong is a sight to behold. It is especially enchanting during sunset and sunrise, when the suns slanting rays paint a glorious picture on the shimmering waters. We camp right on the banks of the lake in our tented huts and spend an enthralling ( and cold! ) night. Overnight at the lake.

Day 6: Pangong - Leh

After a swim in the lake, a small hike up the mountains in search of old Buddhist remains and a hot breakfast, we visit the famed Hemis and Thiksey monasteries. Hemis is the largest monastery in the whole of Ladakh and is situated right on the edge of the Hemis National Park. We might just be able to see some rare trans-Himalayan animals and birds, like the Chukar partridge, Snow cock, Bharal, Pika, Marmot and even the Snow Leopard! We will reach Leh by evening and spend some quality time exploring the old streets of the Ladakhi capital. Overnight in Leh.

Day 7: Leh - Stok - Leh

We drive to Stok, some 15 kms from Leh to see the wonderful Stok palace. Later, we go on a short hike to the Stok Gorge to give you a taste of what trekking is like in Ladakh. This is one of the best day hikes in Ladakh, offering some excellent views and a real workout for your legs! Back to Leh by evening for some last minute shopping. Overnight in Leh.

Day 8: Leh

You will be transferred to the Airport for return journey. Tour ends.
