Sizzling Manali
Simply unforgettable.

Sizzling Manali

6 days / 5 nights
Himachal Pradesh, Chandigarh

Night stays

Chandigarh - 2 nights
Manali - 3 nights

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Chandigarh

Welcome to Chandigarh. We have a short 'Introductory' session before dinner.

Day 2: Chandigarh - Manali

Today we proceed to Manali by enjoying a scenic drive through Pandoh Dam and Kullu Valley. En route we visit Kullu Shawl Factory where you can buy woollen products.

Day 3: Manali - Rohtang Snow Point - Manali

Today we enjoy spectacular drive towards Rohtang snow point. Free time to play and enjoy in the snow. Please Note: Rohtang pass is 51 kms away from Manali. This Pass is not accessible for almost 8 months in a year as it is covered by snow. We generally visit the snow point on Rohtang road, which has been permitted by the Border Security Force. Entertain yourself through spectacular Himachali folk dance.

Day 4: Manali - Solang Valley - Manali

Today we explore the ancient temples like Vashista Kund and Hadimba Mandir. Then we proceed to Solang Valley – A splendid valley between Solang village which offers the view of glaciers and snowcapped mountains. Later we enjoy Ropeway ride, Enjoy the activities like Horse riding, Skiing, Ice skating, Paragliding, mountain bike, snow scooter, Zorbing at your own. Free time for shopping in the evening.

Day 5: Manali - Chandigarh

Today we proceed to Chandigarh - the capital of Punjab and Haryana. En route we visit Vaishnomata Mandir at Kullu. Enjoy get together with your travel companions in the evening.

Day 6: Chandigarh - Departure to Hometown

It is time now to say goodbye to our tour companions. Let us be in contact with each other through Social Media..Tour concludes.
