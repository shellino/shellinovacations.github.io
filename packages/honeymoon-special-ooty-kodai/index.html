Honeymoon Special Ooty Kodai

Honeymoon Special Ooty Kodai

7 days / 6 nights
Tamil Nadu

Night stays

Kodaikanal - 3 nights
Ooty - 3 nights

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Coimbatore - Kodaikanal

Welcome to Coimbatore. Today we have a short 'Introductory' session before dinner.

Day 2: Kodaikanal

Today we explore Kodaikanal . Visit the Kurinji Temple, Coaker's Walk, Pillar Rocks and Moier Point.

Day 3: Kodaikanal

Today we enjoy boat ride in Kodai lake followed by visit to Byrant Park and Green valley view point.

Day 4: Kodaikanal - Ooty

Today we proceed to Ooty. Queen of the Blue Mountains.

Day 5: Ooty

Today we visit Government Botanical Gardens, Doddabetta Peak and enjoy boat ride in Ooty Lake.

Day 6: Ooty - Coonoor - Ooty

Today we proceed to Coonoor - The second largest hill station in the Nilgiris. We visit Sim's Park, A natural garden and Dolphin's nose and an enormous rock formation.

Day 7: Ooty - Coimbatore - Departure to hometown

It is time now to say goodbye to our tour companions. Let us be in contact with each other through Social Media. And meet again on one more memorable tour. See you all..!! Tour concludes.
