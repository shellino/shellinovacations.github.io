Women's Special Kashmir Vaishnodevi
The peace and paradise of nature.

Women's Special Kashmir Vaishnodevi

9 days / 8 nights
Jammu & Kashmir

Night stays

Srinagar (House Boat) - 2 nights
Srinagar - 2 nights
Pahalgam - 2 nights
Katra - 2 nights

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Srinagar

Welcome to Srinagar, the Summer capital of Indian State Jammu and Kashmir as well as the largest city in the Kashmir region. Evening free for relaxation. We have a short 'Introductory' session before dinner.

Day 2: Srinagar - Sonmarg

Today we explore Sonmarg 'Meadow of Gold'.

Day 3: Srinagar

Today we visit Shankaracharya Temple, Island of Char Chinar where you can your photograph yourself in Kashmiri outfits and we enjoy Shikara ride in Dal Lake. Later explore Nishat Bagh-'The garden of pleasure' and Shalimar Bagh-'Abode of Love.

Day 4: Srinagar - Gulmarg

Welcome to Gulmarg, a snow capital of Kashmir which is also known as 'Meadow of Flowers'. En-route we view apple orchards. On arrival at Gulmarg we visit Khilanmarg by the longest Cable Car which takes us to the Affarwat snow point. We also view the picturesque golf course. Later we proceed to Srinagar en route we visit Carpet outlet.

Day 5: Srinagar - Pahalgam

Today we proceed to Pahalgam En route we visit Cricket bat factory, Avantipur ruin and also we pass-by the Pampore Saffron fields.

Day 6: Pahalgam - Chandanwari - Pahalgam

Today we explore Chandanwari to enjoy the magnificent view of Lidder valley. We also visit Aru Valley - a fascinating valley around 15 km from Pahalgam. Once again you can photograph yourself in Kashmiri outfits at various photo studios of Pahalgam.

Day 7: Pahalgam - Katra - Vaishnodevi

Welcome to Katra, the base point of Vaishnodevi. Today we start the trek of Vaishnodevi Temple (a total trek of 28 kms).

Day 8: Vaishnodevi - Katra

Today we arrive from Vaishnodevi. We have free Day for relaxation or leisure activities. Enjoy get together with your travel companions in the evening.

Day 9: Katra - Departure from Jammu

It is time now to say goodbye to our tour companions. Let us be in contact with each other through Social Media, calls…. And meet again on one more memorable tour. See you all..!! Tour concludes.
