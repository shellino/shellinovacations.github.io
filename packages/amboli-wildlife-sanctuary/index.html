Amboli - 2 nights

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Pune - Amboli

Welcome to Pune from where we Proceed towards Amboli. We reach Amboli by lunch-time and over lunch, we discuss the course of action over the next few days. In the evening we leave for our first nature trail! Lots of colourful insects and butterflies, snakes lurk in the dense bushes of Amboli. Post-dinner, we would be going for a short walking trail in search of the Malabar Gliding Frog.

Day 2: Amboli

We spend an entire day in the wild! We would be visiting the dense forest patches of Parikshit point which are famous for their sightings of the Malabar Pit Viper and other beauties from the herp world. In the evening, after a refreshing walk through the forest paths, we visit the glorious Kavlesaad waterfalls and its environs! After a sumptuous dinner, we once again go for a short night trail to explore the magnificent night life of Amboli!

Day 3: Amboli - Pune

This would be our last foray into the jungle of Amboli. Jewel bugs, vine snakes, Malabar Whistling Thrushes are some of Amboli's more famous denizens! The walking trail would be followed by lunch. Post lunch we will move towards Pune for returrn journey. Tour Concludes.
