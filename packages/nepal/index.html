Mystique Nepal
In the womb of Himalayas.

Mystique Nepal

6 days / 5 nights
Uttar Padesh-India/Nava-Nepal/Tamuwan-Nepal

Night stays

Lumbini - 1 nights
Kathmandu - 2 nights
Pokhara - 2 nights

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Gorakhpur – Lumbini

Guest will meet at Gorakhpur Railway station and will proceed toward Lumbini by bus.

Day 2: Lumbini - Kathmandu

Today we visit Kathmandu, Nepals largest urban agglomeration, enroute we will see the famous Kathmandu valleys.

Day 3: Kathmandu

Today we visit famous Taleju Bhawani temple & Garden of dreams.

Day 4: Kathmandu - Pokhara

Today will move towards Pokhara, enroute we will visit the famous Manokamanadevi temple. Overnight stay will be at Pokhara.

Day 5: Pokhara

Today we explore the city Pokhara, we visit Mahendra Gufa & famous Gupteshwar mahadev, Phewa Lake.

Day 6: Pokhara - Gorakpur

Today we will move for return journey towards Gorakhpur. Tour ends at Gorakhpur railway station with good memories. Please be in touch through Social Media.
