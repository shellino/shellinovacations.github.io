Amritsar Dharamshala Dalhousie Vaishnodevi

9 days / 8 nights
Himachal Pradesh, Chandigarh

Night stays

Amritsar - 2 nights
Dharamshala - 2 nights
Dalhousie - 2 nights
Katra - 2 nights

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Amritsar

Welcome to Amritsar home to the Harmandir Sahib, the spiritual and cultural centre for the Sikh religion. Free time for relaxation in evening. Today we have a short 'Introductory' session before dinner.

Day 2: Amritsar

Today we explore the city of Amritsar. We visit Golden temple, Jallianwala bagh, Durgaiana temple and Wagha border. Free time for shopping in the evening.

Day 3: Amritsar - Dharamshala

Welcome to Dharamshala, formerly known as Bhagsu one of the spiritual dwelling in Himachal Pradesh. On arrival we visit Bhagsunag Temple and Dalai Lama Monastery. Later we visit the Dal lake – known for its scenic beauty. Enjoyshopping at Macleodganj Bazaar.

Day 4: Dharamshala

Today we explore the city of Dharamshala. We visit Jwalaji Temple later we explore Kangra Fort and Tapovan.

Day 5: Dharamshala - Dalhousie

Welcome to Dalhousie, located on the western edge of the Dhauladhar mountain range of the Himalayas. It is surrounded by snow-capped peaks. The Dalhousie Town was named after Lord Dalhousie who was the British Governor-General in India. We visit Panchpula and Satdhara Sparkling Springs in the evening.

Day 6: Dalhousie - Khajjiar - Chamba - Dalhousie

Today explore the city of Dalhousie. We proceed to Chamba. Later we visit Khajjiar popularly known as “Mini Switzerland” of India , where you can also enjoy at your own various sports activities like Zorbing. We visit the Chaugan, Laxmi Narayan Temple and Chamunda Devi Temple at Chamba.

Day 7: Dalhousie - Katra - Vaishnodevi

Welcome to Katra, the base point of vaishnodevi . Later proceed to Vaishnodevi Temple, a total trek of 28 km.

Day 8: Vaishnodevi - Katra

Today we arrive from Vaishnodevi. Day free for relaxation or leisure activities.

Day 9: Katra - Departure to hometown

It is time now to say goodbye to our tour companions. Let us be in contact with each other through Social Media and calls…. And meet again on one more memorable tour. See you all !!Tour concludes.
