Traditional Chardham Yatra

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Madurai - Rameshwaram

On arrival to Madurai transfer to Rameshwaram . Visit the temple. Overnight stay at hotel in Rameshwaram.

Day 2: Rameshwaram - Madurai

Take early morning Sphatik darshan. After lunch depart to Madurai. After reaching Madurai , visit the famous Meenakshi temple - Madurai's claim to fame for its meticulous colorful carvings on the Gopurams ( towers). Overnight stay at Madurai.

Day 3: Madurai - Chennai

Transfer to airport to take flight to Chennai. Overnight stay at Chennai.

Day 4: Chennai - Bhubaneshwar - Puri

Take flight from chennai to bhubaneshwar. On arrival transfer to hotel at Puri. Enroute visit to Puri - Konark temple. Relax time at Puri beach. Overnight stay at Puri.

Day 5: Puri - Bhubaneshwar

Board flight to Delhi. On arrival depart to Haridwar. Later arrival at Haridwar. Overnight stay hotel in Haridwar.

Day 6: Bhubaneshwar - Delhi - Haridwar

Board flight to Delhi. On arrival depart to Haridwar. Later arrival at Haridwar. Overnight stay hotel in Haridwar.

Day 7: Haridwar - Rudraprayag

Proceed to Rudraprayag by non a\c mini coach . Arrive at Rudraprayag in the evening . Overnight stay at hotel.

Day 8: Rudraprayag - Badrinath

Proceed to Badrinath by mini non a\c coach, en-route view the 3 Sangams of holy rivers - Vishnuprayag - Alaknanda and Dhauliganga, Nandaprayag- Alaknanda and Mandakini, Karnaprayag - Alaknanda and Pinderganga. Arrive at Badrinath in the evening. Overnight stay at Badrinath.

Day 9: Badrinath

Visit Badrinath Temple, Mana village, Saraswati river, Vyas Gumpha, Ganesh Gumpha, Bhim Bridge & Pandava's Swargarohan Margdarshan. Take a holy dip in Tapt Kund fed by the hot sulphurous springs. Overnight stay at Badrinath.

Day 10: Badrinath - Kirtinagar

Proceed to Kirtinagar. Arrive at Kirtinagar in the evening. Overnight stay at Kirtinagar.

Day 11: Kirtinagar - Haridwar

Proceed to Haridwar . In the evening, Har ki Pawadi and attend Ganga arati. Overnight stay at Haridwar.

Day 12: Haridwar - Delhi

Proceed to Delhi from Haridwar. Overnight stay at Delhi.

Day 13: Delhi - Ahmedabad

Leave for airport take flight to Ahmedabad. In the evening visit Akshardham temple. Overnight stay at Ahmedabad.

Day 14: Ahmedabad - Dwarka

Proceed to Dwarka . Arrive at Dwarka in the evening.

Day 15: Dwarka - Bet Dwarka - Dwarka

Proceed to Bet Dwarka - residence of Lord Shri Krishna. On arrival visit Ranchodas Temple, Dwarkadheesh temple and Rukmini temple. Transfer back to Dwarka.

Day 16: Dwarka - Jamnagar

Proceed to Jamnagar from Dwarka .Tour Concludes.
