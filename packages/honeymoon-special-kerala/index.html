Honeymoon Special Kerala
Reach for the sand and dine on the beach.

Honeymoon Special Kerala

7 days / 6 nights
Kerala

Night stays

Munnar - 2 nights
Periyar - 2 nights
Cochin - 2 nights

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Cochin - Munnar

Welcome to Cochin & proceed towards Munnar. Today we have short 'Introductory' session before dinner.

Day 2: Munnar

Today we enjoy speed boat ride at Mattupetty Dam. View Tea Garden, visit Echo Point and Eravikulam (Rajamalai) National Park.

Day 3: Munnar - Periyar (Thekkady)

Welcome to Periyar. Enjoy the traditional Kairali show in the evening.

Day 4: Periyar (Thekkady)

Today we visit Periyar wildlife Sanctuary by boat in Periyar Lake. Free time for shopping at Kumily Spice Village.

Day 5: Periyar (Thekkady) - Cochin

Welcome to Cochin. En-route enjoy the famous Kerala Backwaters ride from Alleppey to Nedumudi. Later we enjoy live Kathakali dance performance.

Day 6: Cochin

Today we visit St. Francis Church, Jewish Synagogue and Dutch Palace. We Take a Harbour Cruise Ride to see the Chinese Fishing Nets. We have free time for shopping in the evening . Also enjoy get-together with your travel companions.

Day 7: Departure from Cochin

It is time now to say goodbye to our tour companions. Let us be in contact with each other through Social Media. And meet again on one more memorable tour. See you all !! Tour concludes.
