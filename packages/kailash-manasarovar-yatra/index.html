Ultimate test of your fitness.

Kailash Manasarovar Yatra

13 days / 12 nights
Nepal, Tibet

Night stays

Kathmandu - 3 nights
Nyalamu - 3 nights
Saga - 2 nights
Mansarovar - 1 nights
Darchen - 1 nights
Dira Puk - 1 nights
Zutul Puk - 1 nights

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Kathmandu

Arrive at Kathmandu. Proceed to hotel. Later briefing on Kailash Manasarovar Yatra and completing formalities with final preparations. Overnight stay at Hotel.

Day 2: Kathmandu

Vist the famous Hindu Pashupatinath Temple, on the banks of the holy Bagmati river and Bodhnath Temple. Free time for shopping.

Day 3: Kathmandu - Nyalamu

Departure to Nyalamu - 3750 mts. A scenic drive to Tibet. Overnight stay at guest house.

Day 4: Nyalamu

Full day free for weather acclimat-ization. Overnight stay at guest house.

Day 5: Nyalamu - Saga

Departure to Saga - 4850 mts, enroute we cross the Brahmaputra River. Arrive at Saga and Overnight stay at guest house.

Day 6: Saga - Manasarovar

Drive through wild country to Lake Manasarovar - 4560 mts. Drive along the beautiful bluffs of lake Manas to Chiu Gompa, spectacularly situated on northwest shores of the lake. Overnight stay at guest house.

Day 7: Manasarovar - Darchen

Morning free time for Pooja and Parikrama. Arrive at Darchen (4560 mts) and overnight stay at guest house.

Day 8: Darchen - Tarboche - Dira Puk

Drive to Tarboche 8 kms this is starting point of Kailash Parikrama. We start treking 14 kms to Dira Puk, enroute view the Lha Chu river west face of Mount Kailash. Camping in tent.

Day 9: Dira Puk - Dolmala Pass - Zutual Puk

Today we continue Parikrama of 22 kms. Enroute Dolmala Pass - 5650 mts and then to Zuthual Puk - 4770mts. Camping in tent.

Day 10: Zutual Puk - Darchen - Saga

Today we continue Parikrama of 14kms to Darchen. On arrival drive to Paryang - 4560 mts. Today we will complete our Parikrama. Overnight stay at guest house.

Day 11: Saga - Nyalamu

Drive from Paryang to Nyalamu. Arrive at Nyalamu. Overnight stay at guest house.

Day 12: Nyalamu - Kathmandu

Proceed to Kathmandu. We drive to the Friendship Bridge at Zhangmu which spans the Bhote Kosi River and marks the Chinese - Nepalese border. Arrive at Kathmandu.

Day 13: Kathmandu

Day free for shopping. Enjoy get-together with your travel companions in the evening and proceed for return journey.. Tour Concludes..
